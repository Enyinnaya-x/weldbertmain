What are Pipe Spools?

Pipe spools are pre-fabricated sections of piping designed to simplify the construction of complex piping systems. These spool pipe pieces are carefully assembled, cut, welded, and tested in our facility to guarantee high levels of quality and accuracy. They are commonly used to reduce installation time on-site and offer a more efficient method for handling and transporting piping materials.
