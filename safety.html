Safety Policy Statement

WeldBert management is fully committed to providing a safe and healthy workplace for all employees, contractors, and visitors. We integrate HSE principles into every aspect of our operations and continually improve our safety culture.

Compliance

We comply with all relevant Nigerian laws and international standards, including:

  • NUPRC/DPR Regulations
  • NESREA Environmental Standards
  • Factory Act
  • ISO 45001 (Occupational Health & Safety)
  • ISO 14001 (Environmental Management)
  • ASME & API Codes

Site Safety Measures

  • PPE: Mandatory use of helmets, gloves, boots, coveralls, and eye protection.
  • Fire & Gas: Fire extinguishers, gas detectors, and emergency alarms at all work areas.
  • Equipment Checks: Routine inspection and maintenance of welding and lifting equipment.
  • Ventilation: Adequate airflow and fume extraction in confined spaces.

Training & Competence

  • All welders hold current certifications and undergo regular skill assessments.
  • Mandatory HSE induction for all staff and contractors.
  • Periodic safety drills and emergency response exercises.

Risk Management & Incident Response

  • Before Work: Job hazard analysis, permit to work, and toolbox talks.
  • During Work: Continuous monitoring, supervision, and adherence to safe work practices.
  • After Work: Incident reporting, investigation, and corrective actions.

Environmental Protection

  • Proper waste management and disposal procedures.
  • Spill control and containment measures.
  • Commitment to sustainability and pollution prevention.

Safety Performance

  • Over 250,000 man-hours without Lost Time Injury (LTI).
  • Zero fatalities since inception.
  • Continuous improvement in safety KPIs.
